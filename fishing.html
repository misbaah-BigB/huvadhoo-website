Sport fishing

Reef fishing, big game, and night trips — with licensed boats only

The Maldives' deep channels and open-ocean access make it a genuinely good sport fishing destination, not just an afterthought excursion. We match the trip type to your experience level and only work with licensed operators.

Licensed boats only

Every fishing charter we book runs on a licensed, safety-compliant boat with an experienced local crew — we check this ourselves before listing anyone as a partner.

Trip types

Three ways to fish here

Half-day

Reef & Bottom Fishing

Close to shore, relaxed pace — good for beginners, families, or anyone short on time. Snapper, grouper, and reef fish are typical catches.

$70–$150 / person
Full-day

Big Game Trolling

Further offshore chasing tuna, wahoo, and sometimes sailfish or marlin — more physically demanding, best for those with some experience.

$300–$700 / boat
Evening

Night Fishing

A calmer, social trip close to the reef after dark — popular with groups and often includes a simple onboard meal.

$60–$120 / person

What's included

What to expect on board

Gear

Rods, tackle, and bait are typically included in the charter price — we confirm this upfront so you're not caught out on the day.

Crew

An experienced local skipper and crew who know the current fishing grounds — this matters more than the boat itself for a good catch rate.

Your catch

Most resorts and guesthouses will cook your catch for dinner on request — ask us to confirm this with your specific property before the trip.

Group size

Charters are typically priced per boat for big game trips and per person for reef/night trips — we'll clarify which applies to your quote.
