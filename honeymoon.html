Honeymoon & couples

A resort actually built for privacy — not just any 5-star with a honeymoon flyer

Plenty of resorts will slap a "honeymoon package" sticker on a standard room. What actually matters is whether the resort culture, villa layout, and dining are genuinely built around couples. We check that before recommending anything.

What we check

What actually makes a resort "honeymoon-ready"

Adults-only or family-heavy?

Some "romantic" resorts are still full of families in peak season. We flag which are genuinely adults-only versus just marketed that way.

Private dining options

Not every resort offers an actual private dinner on the beach or over water — we confirm what's really available, and at what cost.

Villa privacy

Some water villas sit close together with limited visual privacy. We check spacing and layout, not just the category name.

Honeymoon perks, verified

Decorations, welcome drinks, or a spa credit are common but not universal — we confirm exactly what's included in your specific booking, not assumed as standard.
