About us

A Maldives-based travel concierge, not a booking form with a nice photo

We're based here, we've seen what goes wrong when tourists book blind, and we built this agency specifically to fix that — one clear total price, and honest advice on which island actually fits you.

Why we exist

We started this because too many trips get booked on guesswork

Most first-time visitors to the Maldives make their biggest decisions — resort or guesthouse, which transfer, which island — based on a handful of photos and a price they don't fully understand yet. That's how people end up with a surprise seaplane bill, a resort that doesn't suit their kids' ages, or a guesthouse booking that turns out to be nothing at all.

Huvadhoo Maldives Travel exists to close that gap. We're based in the Maldives, we check every supplier and partner ourselves, and we quote one honest total price before you commit — whether you're booking a $150-a-night guesthouse or a private-island honeymoon suite.

We'd rather send you to the right $150 guesthouse than the wrong $800 resort. That's genuinely the whole business model.
