Diving & snorkeling

Manta rays, channels, and reef walls — matched to your certification and dates

The Maldives is one of the world's great dive destinations — but the right site depends on your certification level, the season, and which atoll you're based in. We match all three before recommending anything.

Certified partners only

Every dive center we work with is PADI or SSI certified — we check this ourselves before listing anyone as a partner. No exceptions, regardless of price.

Certification levels

Where you start depends on experience, not age

Never dived

Discover Scuba

A single supervised introductory dive, no certification needed — good for testing the waters, literally.

Beginner

Open Water Certification

A 3–4 day course leading to your first real certification, valid worldwide.

Certified

Advanced Open Water

Deeper dives, night dives, and navigation — opens up more of the Maldives' channel and drift sites.

Experienced

Rescue & Divemaster

For divers wanting to go further — rescue skills or a path toward working as a dive professional.
