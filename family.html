Family packages

A resort that actually works for your kids' ages — not just any 4-star with a crib

Not every resort is built for children. Lagoon depth, kids' club age ranges, and room configuration matter far more than star rating. Tell us your children's ages and we'll match accordingly.

What matters most

What we actually check before recommending a resort

Lagoon safety

Some house reefs drop off sharply just past the shoreline — not ideal with toddlers. We check depth and current before recommending.

Kids' club age range

Clubs typically run 2–12 or 4–12 — the exact range varies a lot by resort. We confirm it covers your children's ages before booking.

Room configuration

Connecting rooms, family beach villas, or a villa with a second bedroom — not every resort offers all of these.

Meal plan fit

All-inclusive is usually easiest with kids — fewer surprise costs on drinks, snacks, and kids' menus.
