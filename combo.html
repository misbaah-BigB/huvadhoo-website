Our signature trip

Half local island, half resort — the trip most visitors don't know they can book

Most first-time visitors pick one or the other: a resort, or a guesthouse. The Combo splits your stay between both — so you get real local culture and a genuine reef, without paying resort rates for the entire trip.

Why it works

What you actually get from splitting the trip

Lower blended cost

A few nights at guesthouse rates brings your average nightly cost down — letting you afford a nicer resort for the second half than a full-length resort stay would.

Real culture, not just a beach

Local restaurants, everyday island life, and conversations you won't have inside a resort bubble.

A proper reef

Many resorts near Male have weaker house reefs than well-chosen local islands close to good snorkeling sites.

One trip, two moods

Relaxed and social on the local island, then quiet and private at the resort — or the other order, if you'd rather end on the beach.
